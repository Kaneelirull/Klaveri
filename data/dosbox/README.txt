Drop your DOS game .zip or .jsdos files in this folder.
They will automatically appear in the DOSBox game picker.

Supported formats:
  *.zip   — ZIP archive containing DOS executable + dosbox.conf
  *.jsdos — js-dos bundle (from dos.zone or packed with js-dos tools)

File naming tip:
  The filename becomes the display name shown in the UI.
  Use readable names, e.g. "Prince of Persia.zip", "Doom.jsdos"

Where to get games:
  https://dos.zone  — huge free library, download as .jsdos bundles
